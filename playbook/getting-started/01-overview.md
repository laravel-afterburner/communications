## Introduction

The Communications package adds {{ entity_label }}-scoped **Discussions**, optional **Chat log** history, and works alongside platform **Announcements** for keeping members informed.

## Who can use Communications

Any member with a current {{ entity_label }} context can access enabled communication features. Individual areas (discussions, chat log) may require additional permissions.
