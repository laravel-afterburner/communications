## Introduction

The Communications package adds {{ entity_label }}-scoped **Announcements**, **Discussions**, and optional **Chat log** history for keeping members informed.

## Who can use Communications

Any member with a current {{ entity_label }} context can access enabled communication features. Individual areas (announcements, discussions, chat log) may require additional permissions.
