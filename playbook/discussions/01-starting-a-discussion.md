## Overview

Discussions let {{ entity_label }} members exchange messages in threaded conversations—useful for council working groups, committees, or general member Q&A when enabled.

## Steps

1. Open **Communications → Discussions** from the main navigation.
2. Click **New discussion**.
3. Enter a clear subject and your opening message.
4. Post the thread. Members with access can reply.

## Who can participate

Discussion visibility depends on your {{ entity_label }} configuration. Some discussions are limited to council or specific roles; others may be open to all members. If you cannot start or view a discussion, contact an administrator.

## Best practices

- Keep subjects specific so threads stay easy to search later.
- Avoid sharing sensitive personal data in open discussions.
- Use [Announcements](/help/communications/posting-announcements) for one-way broadcasts to all members.
