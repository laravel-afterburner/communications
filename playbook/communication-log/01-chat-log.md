## Overview

The chat log records notification and messaging activity for your {{ entity_label }}, giving administrators an audit-friendly view of outbound communications.

## Steps

1. Open **Chat** from the main navigation.
2. Select **Chat log** (when enabled for your role).
3. Browse or filter entries by date, channel, or recipient as available.
4. Use the log to confirm notices were sent or to troubleshoot delivery questions.

## Permissions

Only members authorized to **view the communication log** for the current {{ entity_label }} can open this area.
